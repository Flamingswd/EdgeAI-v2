Sections Included:

    • Option B: Delay 4 weeks for full scope (Higher cost, risk to market timing)
    • Recommendation: Option A based on competitive pressure analysis
  4. Budget & Resources: Spend vs. budget, resource constraints, funding needs
  5. Risks: Top 2-3 risks with impact and mitigation status
  6. 3-Month Outlook: What's coming next, dependencies, success metrics
